Porsche type 904

the .IE01

The Æ94 Restomod project is a tribute to the Porsche Type-904, an iconic machine that pioneered automotive racing and embodied Porsche’s commitment to a lightweight construction with the use of composite materials, and aerodynamic innovation.

Infomation

On the track and on the road, the 904 sybolized Porsche's early dedication to precision engineering and minimalistic design. By maintaining the 904’s timeless silhouette and distinctive composite body, we bring advanced manufacturing processes and the latest engineering to enhance the 904’s diriving experience.

Born from the desire of creating a vehicle that mixed simplicity and quality without compromises, Æ94 is a departure from the more common Porsche 911 restomods. While the 911 is undoubtedly a legend, the Porsche 904 deserves its own place in history.

Throughout collaborating with leading manufacturers, we ensure that this restomod will not only be a showpice but an exceptional driving experience. A perfectly ballance between understated and thrill full. Every element of Æ94 is meticulously crafted to balance nostalgia with the demands of today’s driving world.

At the heart of Aerfal Automotive lies a commitment to simplicity and undercompromising quality. Æ94 reflects this ethos, where detail serves a purpose in the performance, design, and durability. Our moto, Chasing Perfection, captures the essence of our mision. —To create a car that is not just a tribute to the past, but a modern interpretation of the automotive excellence, setting a new standard for what a classic car can be.
